When You Need a Date-of-Death Appraisal in Highland Park / Mt Washington, Los Angeles

A date-of-death appraisal determines the fair market value of real property as of the date a property owner passed away. If you're handling an estate that includes property in Highland Park / Mt Washington, Los Angeles, you may need this appraisal for establishing the stepped-up cost basis for inherited property, resolving trust distributions among beneficiaries, settling an estate after a loved one passes away, or filing IRS Form 706 (Estate Tax Return).

Our desktop appraisal service provides a USPAP-compliant valuation prepared by a California Certified Residential Appraiser — accepted by the IRS, probate courts, and estate attorneys statewide.
