💡 What Photos Do We Need?

We're looking for photos that give a good general understanding of the overall condition, quality, and features of the property. Any photos that help show that are welcome.

  • Front of home — straight-on view from the street
  • Rear of home — showing the back of the house
  • Backyard — yard, pool, patio, ADU, or other structures
  • Kitchen — countertops, cabinets, appliances, and flooring
  • Bathrooms — at least the primary bathroom, including flooring
  • Living areas — living room, family room, dining room, including flooring
  • Views — if the property has a view, show it with context: through a window with the frame visible, or from a deck or yard with the deck or yard in the picture
  • Any condition issues — deferred maintenance, damage, repairs

Take photos in landscape with good lighting. If your phone has a wide-angle camera, use it — it captures more of the room in a single shot. More photos are always better — we'll use what we need.
