What Photos Do We Need?

  • Front of home — straight-on view from the street
  • Street scene — view looking down the street in each direction
  • Backyard — showing the yard and any structures (pool, patio, ADU)
  • Kitchen — showing countertops, cabinets, and appliances
  • Bathrooms — at least the primary bathroom
  • Living areas — living room, family room, dining room
  • Any recent upgrades — new flooring, roof, HVAC, windows, solar panels
  • Any condition issues — deferred maintenance, damage, needed repairs

Tip: Take photos in landscape orientation with good lighting. More photos are always better — we'll use what we need.
